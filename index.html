Introduction

The debate over the Fighter of the Year for 2024 has sparked discussions among MMA fans and experts alike. Two names stand out: Ilia Topuria and Alex Pereira. Both have had stellar years, but when analyzing their achievements in-depth, it becomes clear that Topuria’s accomplishments overshadow Pereira’s in terms of quality.

Achievements in 2024

Ilia Topuria

  • Knockout victory over Alexander Volkanovski to claim the Featherweight Championship.
  • First title defense with a second-round knockout against Max Holloway, a featherweight legend.

Alex Pereira

  • Won the Light Heavyweight Championship on short notice at UFC 300.
  • Defended the title three times, defeating Jamahal Hill, Jiří Procházka, and Khalil Rountree.

While Pereira’s activity is commendable, his opponents—aside from Jiří Procházka—do not match the legendary status of Volkanovski and Holloway. Topuria’s wins are not just victories; they are monumental achievements against two of the greatest featherweights of all time.

Why Not Alexandre Pantoja?

Alexandre Pantoja is another name that deserves recognition. With three successful title defenses in the Flyweight division, he has proven himself to be a dominant champion. However, the Flyweight division remains one of the most underrated in the UFC, and this often impacts the visibility of its champions.

Pantoja’s achievements are incredible, but the lack of marquee opponents or high-profile events compared to Topuria and Pereira places him just below them in the Fighter of the Year discussion.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Why does Ilia Topuria deserve Fighter of the Year?

Topuria's victories over Alexander Volkanovski and Max Holloway stand out due to the caliber of his opponents, both of whom are considered legends in the featherweight division. His accomplishments define quality over quantity.

What makes Alex Pereira a strong contender?

Pereira’s success in winning and defending the Light Heavyweight title on short notice, along with his activity level, makes him a compelling candidate. However, the quality of his opponents does not rival Topuria's.

Why isn’t Alexandre Pantoja more recognized?

Despite defending the Flyweight title three times, Pantoja competes in a division that often gets overlooked. While his achievements are remarkable, they lack the same visibility as those of Topuria and Pereira.

What criteria determine Fighter of the Year?

Typically, factors like quality of opponents, number of victories, title wins and defenses, and overall impact on the sport are considered. Topuria excels in these metrics, particularly in the quality of his opposition.

Can Pereira still win the award?

Absolutely. If activity and the number of fights defended weigh more heavily for some analysts, Pereira could take the honor. However, Topuria's victories resonate more in terms of legacy and impact.

Conclusion: The Case for Ilia Topuria

Ilia Topuria’s 2024 campaign stands out not just for its quality but for the magnitude of his victories. Beating Alexander Volkanovski, widely regarded as one of the greatest featherweights of all time, and defending against Max Holloway, a living legend, is an unparalleled feat.

Alex Pereira undoubtedly had a remarkable year, stepping in on short notice and defending his title three times. However, the level of competition he faced does not compare to the legendary status of Topuria’s opponents.

As fans, we recognize Pereira’s contributions, but as professionals analyzing the impact, it is clear that 2024 belongs to Ilia Topuria.
